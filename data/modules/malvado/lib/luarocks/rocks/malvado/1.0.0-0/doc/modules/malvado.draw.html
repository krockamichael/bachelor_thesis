set_color (red, green, blue, alpha)
Sets the color of the next primitive to be painted. It's an alias of: love.graphics.setColor.

Parameters:

  • red Red
  • green Green
  • blue Blue
  • alpha Alpha (Optional: Default 255)
draw_circle (x0, y0, radius)

Parameters:

  • x0 X position
  • y0 Y position
  • radius Circle Radius

Parameters:

  • x0 X position

draw_box (x0, y0, x1, y1)
Draws a filled rectangle with corners (x0,y0), (x0,y1), (x1,y0) and (x1,y1).

Parameters:

  • x0 The x coordinate of one corner of the filled rectangle.
  • y0 The y coordinate of one corner of the filled rectangle.
  • x1 The x coordinate of the diagonally opposite corner of the filled rectangle.
  • y1 The y coordinate of the diagonally opposite corner of the filled rectangle.
draw_rect (x0, y0, x1, y1)
Draws a non filled rectangle with corners (x0,y0), (x0,y1), (x1,y0) and (x1,y1).

Parameters:

  • x0 The x coordinate of one corner of the filled rectangle.
  • y0 The y coordinate of one corner of the filled rectangle.
  • x1 The x coordinate of the diagonally opposite corner of the filled rectangle.
  • y1 The y coordinate of the diagonally opposite corner of the filled rectangle.
